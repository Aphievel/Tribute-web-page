Tim Berners-Lee

The inventor of the World Wide Web

Here's a timeline for Tim Berners-Lee's Life

  • 1955 - Born in London, England
  • 1969 - Joined south-west London's Emanuel School
  • 1973 - Joined The Queen's College, Oxford
  • 1976 - Graduates with a first-class Bachelor of Arts degree in physics and joins the telecommunications company Plessey as an engineer
  • 1978 - Joins D. G. Nash where he helps create typesetting software for printers.
